What is DigiBlooms, and how does it work?

DigiBlooms is a web-based platform that combines the principles of personal finance with an eco-conscious and gamified experience. Users can nurture their digital garden by making deposits, setting savings goals, and earning points for rewards. The unique twist is that withdrawals are only allowed once users reach their savings goals.
